# How to Connect with OpenLumara 🔌

You have many ways to talk to your AI assistant! Depending on where you are and what you prefer, you can use different 'Channels'.

A **Channel** is simply the interface you use to send messages and receive replies. Some popular ones include:

- **Web UI**: A beautiful, easy-to-use website.
- **Telegram**: Chat with your AI right from your phone!
- **Discord**: Perfect if you're already hanging out in Discord servers.
- **CLI**: For those who love the classic terminal look.

**Pro Tip:** You can customize how each channel works! For example, you can change settings through the Web UI or by using the `/config` command. This lets you tailor your experience to exactly how you like it. ✨
