# Navigating the Sidebar 🧭

The sidebar is your command center. It holds your chat history, your categories, and your settings.

## Finding Your Way
- **The Chat List**: This shows all your previous conversations. You can click any chat to jump straight into it.
- **Categories**: Your chats are organized into groups (like "General" or specific "Characters"). This helps you keep different topics separate.
- **The Category Strip**: On the far left, you'll find a slim strip of icons. This lets you quickly switch between different groups of chats.

## Hiding the Sidebar
If you want more room to chat, you can hide the sidebar!
- **On Desktop**: You can toggle the sidebar on and off. Lumara will remember your preference, so it stays hidden the next time you log in.
- **On Mobile**: The sidebar is hidden by default to save space. You can swipe from the left edge of your screen to bring it out.

## Quick Tips
- **Drag and Drop**: On a computer, you can actually grab a chat and drop it into a different category to organize it!
- **Search**: Use the search icon at the top of the sidebar to find a specific chat by name or even by words inside the conversation.
