# Personalizing Your Experience 🎨

Lumara is your companion, so it should look and feel exactly how you like! You can customize almost every part of the interface.

## Appearance & Themes
- **Color Themes**: Choose from a variety of beautiful color palettes. Whether you like a bright, clean look or a deep, dark aesthetic, there's a theme for you.
- **Dark & Light Mode**: Easily switch between Dark and Light modes to suit your environment or your eyes.

## Typography (Fonts)
- **Font Family**: Pick a font that is easy for you to read. We have a huge selection, including beautiful Google Fonts.
- **Font Size**: Make the text larger for easier reading or smaller to see more of the conversation at once.
- **Chat Width**: Adjust how wide the chat bubbles appear on your screen.

## Sound Effects
For a more immersive experience, you can enable sound effects:
- **Typewriter Sound**: Hear the satisfying "click-clack" of text appearing as Lumara types.
- **Completion Sound**: A gentle chime to let you know Lumara has finished its thought.
- **Volume Control**: You can adjust the volume or even mute the sounds entirely.

## How to Change Settings
Click the **Settings** icon (the gear) in your menu. From there, you can explore all these options. Most changes happen instantly, but some (like changing modules) might require a quick server restart to take effect.
