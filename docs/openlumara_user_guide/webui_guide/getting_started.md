# Welcome to OpenLumara! ✨

Getting started with your new AI companion is easy. Lumara is designed to be as seamless as possible, so you can focus on your conversations rather than technical setups.

## Automatic Connection
The moment you open the WebUI, Lumara begins working to establish a connection with the server. You don't need to click any "Connect" buttons—it happens automatically in the background.

## What if the connection drops?
Don't worry! If your internet flickers or the server takes a break, Lumara will notice. You'll see a small notification in your chat telling you it's "Reconnecting..." 

Lumara will keep trying to find its way back automatically. Once it's back online, it will let you know, and you can pick up exactly where you left off.

## Pro Tip
If you ever feel stuck or the screen seems unresponsive, simply refreshing your browser page will trigger a fresh connection attempt!
