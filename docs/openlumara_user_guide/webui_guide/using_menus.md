# Using Menus & Pop-ups 🖱️

Sometimes, Lumara will show you a pop-up window (a "modal") to ask for your input or show you settings.

## How to Interact
- **Clicking Around**: Most menus allow you to click anywhere outside the window to close it and go back to your chat.
- **The Escape Key**: If you ever feel stuck in a menu, just hit the `Esc` key on your keyboard. It's the fastest way to close any pop-up.

## Common Menus
- **Settings**: Where you can change your themes, fonts, and sounds.
- **Search**: A dedicated window to help you find specific words or chats.
- **Export**: A way to save your conversations for later.

## A Helpful Note on Saving
When you are changing important settings (like how Lumara's "brain" works), Lumara might ask: *"You have unsaved changes. Close without saving?"* 
Always make sure to click **Save** if you want your new settings to stick!
