# Chats

Chats are the way openlumara stores your chat history. It comes with a few powerful organisation features, and it's designed to work across all types of channels, though the most premier experience is in the WebUI.

Thanks to the `chats` module, you can simply just ask your AI to rename, tag, or categorize your chat. Your AI can later find older chats by tag, name, or even search in their content. In the WebUI, categories and tags are *really* useful because you can very quickly filter down using the category bar (check the webui docs) and the chat list. The chat list also lets you search through your chats and filter by tag. 

To use the chat system in channels that are not the webui, you can use `/chats` to see your most recent chats. This will show a list with an ID to the side. You can use that ID in the `/chat` command to load that chat's history and resume where you left off! Make sure to type or paste that ID without the square brackets.
